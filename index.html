SATORARE

onchain registry for sats of value

"Satorare" (悟られ) describes a state of being understood or realized. Sudden, unpredictable moments of enlightenment or realization. Related to "Satori".This may be experienced as we discover sats of value.Anything can be anchored to an Ordinal sat resulting in a digital artifact with permanence and durability on the Bitcoin blockchain.Satorare is an onchain registry for sats of value. Curated collections and exhibits will be derived from the data in the registry. Marketplaces and Wallet integrations will result. A historical record of Bitcoin as a store of and for value as perceived, assigned and approved by a community of curators.Learn more in the Satorare Registry Github.
